Hazardous Areaa Classification(HAC)

Maximize your risk management strategies and regulatory compliance with Capeserve Energy's comprehensive Hazardous Area Classification services. We don't just offer solutions; we forge partnerships in guaranteeing safety and success at every project stage.

Ignition Hazard Risk Assessment(IHA)

Under ATEX 2014/34/EU, the approach for Mechanical (Non-Electrical) Equipment varies from the conformity assessment route for Electrical Equipment. Since 2016, it is also possible to perform evaluation and certification under the IECEx Scheme, with the recent ExMC 15 decision and publication of the ISO/EN 80079-36 and 80079-37 standards.

Hazardous Area Design Review

Hazardous Area Design Review is a critical process that assesses the design of installations and equipment in areas where there is a risk of explosion or fire.

Hazardous Area Verification Dossier

A Hazardous Area Verification Dossier is a crucial collection of documents that demonstrates compliance with safety standards and regulations for hazardous locations.
